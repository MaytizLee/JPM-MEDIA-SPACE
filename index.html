Extend Interactivity

Add-on Sensor Technologies

Integrate secondary sensory inputs to build complex, immersive multi-dimensional experiences.

LiDAR Scanning

Laser-based distance tracking. Enables multi-point touch overlays on giant walls or ground projections up to 30 meters wide.

Depth Cameras

Skeleton and gesture mapping. Captures user body shapes to control digital graphics or trigger actions with custom hand gestures.

RFID & NFC

Physical token triggers. Allows visitors to tap physical cards, badges, or smart tokens on tables to trigger personalized display content.
